About Me

Trisha Croatt is a driven and creative full-stack web developer with skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, JQuery, and Foundation. She is dedicated to giving her clients the perfect web experience with the latest in funtional website design. She recieved a full-stack developer certificate from the University of Denver, and continues to research and learn on her own. In her free time, Trisha writes speculative fiction under the name Trisha Lea. She lives in Colorado Springs with her partner, two kids, two cats, and house dingo (aka Australian cattle dog mix).
